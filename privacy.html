Privacy Policy

Last updated: 30 May 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Indian Food Nutrition Calculator ("we", "the tool") handles your information when you use our website at indian-food-nutrition.vercel.app.

1. No Personal Data Collected

The Indian Food Nutrition Calculator does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All nutrition calculations are performed entirely in your browser using JavaScript. No food searches, meal plans, or usage data are sent to any server.

2. No Account Required

You do not need to create an account, provide an email address, or log in to use any feature of this tool.

3. Local Storage

This tool does not currently use browser localStorage or cookies to store your data. Your meal builder data exists only in memory and is cleared when you close or refresh the page.

4. Analytics

We may use privacy-respecting analytics (such as Google Analytics with IP anonymisation enabled) to understand aggregate usage patterns — for example, which pages are most visited. This data is anonymous and cannot be used to identify individual users.

5. Advertising

This site may display advertisements via Google AdSense. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website or other websites. You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

6. Third-Party Links

This site does not contain links to third-party websites that collect personal data.

7. Children's Privacy

This tool is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
